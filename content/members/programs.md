## Education Assistance

Financial assistance for enrolled members pursuing higher education, including tuition support, book allowances, and living stipends.

**Eligibility:** Enrolled tribal members attending accredited institutions

[Learn more about Education](/department/education)

## Housing Programs

Housing assistance programs including home rehabilitation, emergency repairs, and rental assistance for qualified tribal members.

**Contact:** Housing Department

[Learn more about Housing](/department/housing)

## Emergency Assistance

Short-term emergency assistance for tribal members facing unexpected hardships including utilities assistance, food assistance, and emergency travel.

**Contact:** Social Services Department

## Elder Services

Programs supporting tribal elders including meal delivery, transportation assistance, and wellness check-ins.

**Age requirement:** 55 years and older

## Youth Programs

Summer youth employment, cultural education programs, sports activities, and after-school tutoring for tribal youth.

**Ages:** 5-18 years old

## Health Services

Access to Toiyabe Indian Health Project services, wellness programs, and health education resources.

**Location:** Big Pine Health Clinic

[Learn more about Health Services](/department/health)

---

## How to Apply

To apply for any of these programs, please contact the relevant department or visit the Tribal Administration office during business hours. Most programs require proof of tribal enrollment.
